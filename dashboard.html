ABOUT

India is a country in South Asia. It is the second-most populous country, the seventh-largest country by land area, and the most populous democracy in the world. Bounded by the Indian Ocean on the south, the Arabian Sea on the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal on the southeast, it shares land borders with Pakistan to the west; China, Nepal, and Bhutan to the north; and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. In the Indian Ocean, India is in the vicinity of Sri Lanka and the Maldives; its Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime border with Thailand, Myanmar and Indonesia. India celebrates its independence day every year on 15th August.

ABOUT THE GAME

The game is built keeping in mind about the ethics of India. The game encourages young people to take interest in our land and its stories. In the quizzes, there will be different rounds. Once you start, there will be questions of varied range. Suppose if I consider the India Gate. Where is it located? Who built it? Why it was built? Then places like Sundarbans where we find open flora and fauna, the importance of such places to be preserved. With all these, we will try to cover the geography and the history related to it. Then as you complete one round, the next round focuses on the culture and diversity. Our ancient arts, music and literature. The realization that each individual is unique and holds their unique perspectives.

Most of us grow up with loads of black and white books and consequently lose our interest in studying about our own country but what if it is all a kind of an interesting game!
